2. **Legitimate Interests (Article 6(1)(f)):** To improve the Service, ensure security, and conduct analytics
3. **Consent (Article 6(1)(a)):** For optional features (marketing emails, non-essential cookies) — you may withdraw consent at any time
4. **Legal Obligation (Article 6(1)(c)):** To comply with UK laws, regulations, or legal processes (e.g., Companies Act 2006 record retention)

For UK users, legitimate interests are balanced against your rights and freedoms under UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f).

## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Retention Periods

**Active Accounts:**
- Account data: Retained while your account is active
- Flight records: Retained indefinitely (unless you delete them)
- Chat messages: Retained for 2 years (or until you delete your account)
- Weather cache: Retained for 24 hours (then automatically purged)
- Airport cache: Retained for 30 days (then refreshed or purged)

- If you do not log in for **24 months**, we may delete your account after email notice

**Deleted Accounts:**
- Account data deleted within **30 days** of termination request
- Some data retained for **90 days** in backups (then permanently deleted)
- Legal retention: Data may be retained longer if required by law (e.g., tax records, legal disputes)

### 6.2 Right to Request Early Deletion

You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 9 for data subject rights).
