This comprehensive airport risk assessment data was collected through systematic deep web research across authoritative aviation sources including:

2. **Official Airport Resources**
   - Airport official websites and operational documents
   - FBO (Fixed Base Operator) service providers
   - Airport management publications

3. **Aeronautical Information Publications (AIP)**
   - AIP Germany (Luftfahrthandbuch Deutschland)
   - AIP UK (United Kingdom Aeronautical Information Publication)
   - AIP Austria (Luftfahrthandbuch Österreich)

4. **Industry Databases and Publications**
   - EUROCONTROL network operations data
   - Slots coordination databases
   - Aviation safety databases (SKYbrary)
   - Performance review reports
